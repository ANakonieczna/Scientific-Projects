The program solves the equations describing gravitational collapse of matter within the field theory involving the Higgs field, dark matter, dilaton and the Gauss-Bonnet coupling, using the Adams-Bashforth-Moulton and Runge-Kutta methods, as well as the adaptive mesh refinement algorithm.

Reference: European Physical Journal C 80 (2020) 1051; European Physical Journal C 82 (2022) 294.
